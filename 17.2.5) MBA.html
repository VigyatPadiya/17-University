MBA Subjects Information

Marketing Management

Explore strategies for product promotion, market research, branding, and customer behavior analysis.

Financial Accounting

Learn the fundamentals of financial reporting, analysis, and management, including budgeting and investment decisions.

Operations Management

Study efficient processes for production, supply chain management, quality control, and project management.

Human Resource Management

Focus on workforce planning, recruitment, training, employee relations, and organizational behavior.

Business Ethics and Corporate Governance

Examine ethical considerations in business decisions and explore corporate governance practices.

Strategic Management

Develop skills in formulating business strategies, competitive analysis, and long-term planning.

Entrepreneurship and Innovation

Explore concepts of creating and managing new ventures, innovation, and business development.

Managerial Economics

Study economic principles and their application to managerial decision-making and market analysis.
