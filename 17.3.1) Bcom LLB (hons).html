BCom LLB (Hons) Subjects

  1. Contract Law

    Contract Law focuses on the study of legally binding agreements and their enforcement. Students learn about contract formation, terms, breach, remedies, and more.

  2. Taxation Law

    Taxation Law covers the legal aspects of taxation, including income tax, corporate tax, indirect taxes, tax planning, and compliance with tax regulations.

  3. Constitutional Law

    Constitutional Law explores the fundamental principles of a country's constitution, the distribution of powers, fundamental rights, and the role of the judiciary.

  4. Business Economics

    Business Economics examines economic concepts and principles in the context of business decisions, including demand and supply, market structures, and pricing strategies.

  5. Criminal Law

    Criminal Law delves into the study of crimes, their elements, defenses, and procedures. Students learn about different types of offenses and their consequences.

  6. Corporate Law

    Corporate Law focuses on legal aspects of business organizations, governance, corporate finance, mergers and acquisitions, and corporate compliance.
