BBA (Financial Markets)

BBA (Financial Markets) is a specialized program that focuses on the study of financial markets, investments, and related concepts. This program prepares students for careers in the financial industry by providing them with a strong foundation in finance, economics, and market analysis.

Program Subjects

  1. Financial Management

    Study of financial planning, budgeting, and management of financial resources within organizations.

  2. Investment Analysis

    Examination of various investment options, risk assessment, and strategies for portfolio management.

  3. Capital Markets

    Exploration of the functioning of capital markets, including stocks, bonds, derivatives, and market regulations.

  4. Financial Reporting

    Understanding financial statements, analysis of financial performance, and reporting standards.

  5. Derivatives and Risk Management

    Study of derivative instruments, hedging strategies, and risk management techniques.

  6. International Finance

    Analysis of global financial markets, exchange rates, and international investment strategies.
