BBA (Business Analysis) Subjects

The BBA (Business Analysis) program is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to analyze business processes, gather data, and make informed decisions. The program covers a range of subjects related to business analysis and management.

Core Subjects

  • Introduction to Business Analysis
  • Statistics for Business
  • Financial Accounting
  • Business Communication
  • Management Principles
  • Marketing Management
  • Operations Management
  • Business Ethics and Corporate Social Responsibility

Specialized Subjects

  • Data Analysis and Interpretation
  • Market Research and Consumer Behavior
  • Project Management
  • Business Process Modeling
  • IT for Business Analysis
  • Business Decision Modeling
  • Strategic Management
  • Enterprise Systems

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the BBA (Business Analysis) program can pursue various roles in the business and technology sectors, including:

  • Business Analyst
  • Data Analyst
  • Market Research Analyst
  • Project Manager
  • Operations Manager
  • Business Intelligence Analyst
  • Management Consultant
