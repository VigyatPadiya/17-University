BBA (Hons) Subjects

  • Management Principles

    Learn the foundational principles of management, including planning, organizing, leading, and controlling within organizations.

  • Marketing Fundamentals

    Explore the basics of marketing, including market research, consumer behavior, branding, and product promotion.

  • Financial Accounting

    Gain an understanding of accounting principles, financial statements, and reporting for business decision-making.

  • Business Ethics

    Examine ethical issues and dilemmas in business contexts, and develop a strong ethical foundation for decision-making.

  • Business Law

    Learn about legal aspects of business, contracts, intellectual property, and regulations affecting business operations.

  • Organizational Behavior

    Study individual and group behavior within organizations, and explore topics such as motivation, communication, and teamwork.
