BCA Subjects Information

  1. Business Web Development (BWD)

    2. Business Web Development is a course that focuses on creating and maintaining websites for businesses. Students learn about web design, front-end and back-end development, and e-commerce integration.

  2. Principles of Programming (PP)

    3. Principles of Programming introduces students to the fundamentals of programming logic and problem-solving. It covers topics like algorithms, data structures, and different programming paradigms.

  3. Environmental Studies (ES)

    4. Environmental Studies is a subject that explores various environmental issues and their impact on society. It covers topics like ecology, conservation, pollution, sustainable development, and environmental policies.

  4. Advanced Operating Systems (AOC)

    5. AOC delves into the design, implementation, and management of complex operating systems. Students study topics like process synchronization, memory management, and file systems.

  5. Mathematics

    6. Mathematics in BCA covers various mathematical concepts relevant to computer science, such as discrete mathematics, linear algebra, and probability theory.

These subjects are part of the BCA curriculum and provide students with a well-rounded understanding of both technical and environmental aspects.
